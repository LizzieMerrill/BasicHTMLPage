My Five Favorite Phoenix Restaurants

Below is a list of my favorite restaurants in all of Phoenix.
Some of them are pretty niche, like Jewel's Cafe (my favorite restaurant of all time),
and I like all of them for different reasons. But you should give them a go!

Jewel's Cafe is catered to people who eat gluten free but still tastes amazing,
2 Fat Guys Grilled Cheese is just super good food for a small business,
Snakes and Lattes is for a fun night out with friends at a board game cafe,
Alien Donuts has super yummy desserts such as milkshakes and donuts, and
Barrio Cafe has all sorts of authentic, amazing-tasting Mexican food!

Welp, enjoy these fresh picks on your next trip to Phoenix!

  1. Jewel's Cafe

  2. 2 Fat Guys Grilled Cheese

  3. Snakes & Lattes

  4. Alien Donuts
  5. Barrio Cafe
